Band Biography:

We are YES DEVIL , a hard rock band based out of the San Francisco Bay Area. Our sound is shaped by a wide range of influences from a variety of genres, from blues to heavy metal. We just finished recording our first full-length studio album at Earthtone Studios, so stay tuned for some new music!
