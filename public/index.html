Band Biography

We are YES DEVIL , a band based out of the San Francisco Bay Area. Consisting of four members that all live together in an RV, we dedicate our lives to the pursuit of freedom, love, and rock 'n' roll. Our sound is shaped by a wide range of influences from a variety of genres, from blues to heavy metal. We just finished recording our first full-length studio album at Earthtone Studios, so stay tuned for some new music!

-Members-

Logan Barsell - Vocals and Guitar

Matt Abbott - Lead Guitar

Eyan Stran - Drums
